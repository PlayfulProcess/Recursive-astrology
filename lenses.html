One house or planet, four traditions

Pick a house or a planet and read how four traditions treat the same chart feature, side by side, in chronological order — Ptolemy's 2nd-century natural philosophy, the Victorian skeptic, the Theosophical revival, and today's flagship interpretation set. Evolution across traditions is the point: where a tradition has no entry (the outer planets didn't exist for Ptolemy), the gap is shown honestly, not papered over. Back to the library.
