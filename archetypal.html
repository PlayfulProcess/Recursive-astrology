Tap any two planets above — the relationship between them is where Tarnas's whole method starts. Every tradition that ever read the sky agreed on planetary relationships mattering, even when they disagreed about everything else.

How this works. Tapping two planets looks up their pair in Archetypal Astrology — planetary pairs (hand-authored, not generated) and stacks it above each planet's own entries from every other voice grammar in this repo — Planetary Myths, Ptolemy's Tetrabiblos, Lilly's Christian Astrology, the Jyotiṣa Bṛhat Jātaka, Alan Leo, and the Canonical synthesis — read live from grammars/_collection.json, the same cross-grammar matching the Lenses viewer uses. This is the meta layer: not a second generator script, but the parents' many voices stacking beneath the pair's own synthesis, live, on every load. See docs/DESIGN-archetypal.md.
