How this works. This course has no separate content of its own — each chapter is read live, client-side, from grammars/historiographies-of-astrology/grammar.json, the same grammar that backs the library. Adding a new era to that grammar automatically adds a new chapter here; nothing in this page needs to change. See course/manifest.json for the course-level config (chapter ordering prefix, companion-grammar links, section order).
